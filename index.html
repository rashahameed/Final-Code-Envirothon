Description

Envirogram is a platform created for needing more greenery in their respective cities. The project lets you know where areas which need trees to be planted are and encourages people to plant there. Every tree planted will be connected with an individual’s ID, which they can share on social media to spread the word about our platform and create a sort of ripple effect. Through forest monitoring, we can recognize which areas might need to be greener,and encourage the people to plant on those areas. Through the app, people will not only fix the bad pollution rates through planting, but will also become more ecological-friendly, wanting to protect the environment.
